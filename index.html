The 1981 Escape.

In 1981, our father fled East Germany. He left everything behind, but he brought his family's recipes.

In 2011, sisters Caroline Lott and Katy Harmer decided those recipes needed to be shared. With no funding, we put our car up as collateral to build Das Café from the ground up.

We aren't a commercial kitchen. We are a family sharing our heritage in the heart of Spring City. From the handmade pottery on our walls to the dill gravy on our Bratwurst, every detail is crafted with warmth and unapologetic authenticity.
